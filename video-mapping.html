Video Mapping

Architectural Projection Art

This video mapping project involved creating large-scale projections on architectural surfaces, transforming buildings into dynamic canvases of light and motion. I worked with a team to design custom animations that were mapped onto the building's geometry.

My contributions included animating visual content, handling the technical aspects of projection mapping, and ensuring the projections were synchronized with the music and other sensory elements to create a cohesive narrative. The result was a captivating, immersive experience for the audience.
